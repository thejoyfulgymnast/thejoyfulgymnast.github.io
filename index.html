About

The story so far

Nora Greenfield is a 12-year-old competitive gymnast training at WOGA Gymnastics under coaches Evgeniya Artemova and Eddie Vavilov. Having completed Level 8 and the Hopes (11–12) division, Nora is now training Level 9 and will compete in the Hopes (13–14) category this summer, continuing her path toward Junior Elite with qualification targeted for early 2027.

Nora's gymnastics journey began at age three, when her mother noticed her natural flexibility and enrolled her in a recreational class. By age six, a coach invited her to join a competitive team, and she began competing Levels 2 and 3 at SGA in North Carolina. In June 2021, Nora's family relocated to Texas, settling less than a mile from WOGA Gymnastics — where she has trained ever since.

A standout on bars, Nora trains more than 30 hours per week and has earned numerous state and regional titles, along with multiple appearances on America's Top 100. Her long-term goals include competing at the NCAA level and representing the United States in international competition.

Outside the gym, Nora is a 7th grader at Texas Connections Academy. She's a Christian, enjoys swimming, and loves spending time with family and friends.
